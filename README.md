## Overview

Taleem AI is a comprehensive AI-powered learning platform designed specifically for **Pakistani students**. It bridges the gap between rote memorisation and genuine conceptual understanding through cutting-edge artificial intelligence, tailored to Pakistan's education system, syllabus, and linguistic needs.

The platform features a **voice-first bilingual interface** (English and Urdu), a **curriculum-grounded RAG system** that answers questions strictly from official textbooks, a complete **smart study suite**, a full **gamification engine** with six educational games, and a **personalised dashboard** for tracking progress.

## Features

### Voice-First Bilingual Interface

Students interact naturally by speaking in English or Urdu. The platform supports full voice-based Q&A, navigation, and content generation, making advanced technology accessible to users of all technical backgrounds. Voice recognition and synthesis work seamlessly across both languages, allowing students to ask questions, request notes, or navigate the platform entirely hands-free.

  <img src="resources/1 (4).png" alt="Voice interface interaction" width="45%" style="border-radius: 8px;"/>
  <img src="resources/1 (5).png" alt="Voice Q&A session" width="45%" style="border-radius: 8px;"/>
</p>

### Curriculum-Grounded RAG Tutoring

Unlike generic AI tools, Taleem AI knowledge base is built exclusively from official Pakistani curriculum textbooks, board papers, and user-uploaded materials. The retrieval-augmented generation system ensures answers are accurate, contextually relevant, and strictly grounded in provided sources, eliminating hallucination and off-topic responses.

### Smart Study Suite

Upload any academic document and instantly generate study materials across multiple formats:

- **AI Notes** with concept maps, diagrams, and key takeaways
- **Interactive Quizzes** with real-time scoring and performance analytics
- **Smart Flashcards** with swipe-based review and spaced repetition
- **Professional Presentations** with slide-by-slide generation and formatting
- **Past Paper Analysis** identifying recurring exam question patterns, topic weightage, and frequently tested concepts

A complete gamification system designed to maintain student motivation and make studying enjoyable:

|---|---|

| Achievement Badges | Unlock milestones for streaks, quiz scores, and content creation |
| Daily Streaks | Consecutive study day tracking with rewards |
| Points System | Earn points for completing activities, quizzes, and study sessions |
| Leaderboards | Subject-specific and overall rankings for friendly competition |

## Educational Games

Six interactive games that reinforce learning through play. Each game targets different cognitive skills and subject areas:

| Game | Focus | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Map Master | Geography | Map-based challenges covering physical and political geography |
| Subject Sprint | General Knowledge | Fast-paced quiz game across all academic subjects |
| Vocabulary Voyage | Language Learning | Vocabulary building through contextual word challenges |
| Timeline Tank | History | Historical event sequencing and chronological ordering |
| Urdu Hero | Urdu Literacy | Urdu language grammar, vocabulary, and comprehension |
| Science Detective | Scientific Reasoning | Experiment-based puzzles and scientific inquiry challenges |
